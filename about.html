About us

Simplifying education loan planning

EduLoan Planner is a free tool designed to help Indian students understand and plan their education loans effectively.

Our Mission

Education is a gateway to opportunities, but often comes with the burden of loans. Our mission is to empower students with clear, easy-to-understand tools that help them make informed financial decisions about their education.

We believe that financial planning shouldn't be complicated. EduLoan Planner provides instant EMI calculations, grace period analysis, and repayment comparisons—all in one place.

What We Offer

  • EMI Calculator: Instantly calculate your monthly education loan repayments in INR
  • Grace Period Analysis: Understand how moratoriums affect your loan balance
  • Scenario Comparison: Compare up to 3 different loan scenarios side-by-side
  • Flexible Repayment Options: Test interest-only periods and advance payments
  • Timezone Converter: Coordinate across different regions effortlessly

Important Note

Disclaimer: EduLoan Planner is a planning tool only and does not constitute financial advice. Always consult with qualified financial advisors or your lender before making decisions about your education loans. The calculations provided are estimates based on standard EMI formulas and may vary based on your bank's specific terms.

Privacy & Data

All calculations are performed locally in your browser. We do not store, transmit, or collect any of your personal financial information. Scenario data is saved only to your browser's local storage and is never sent to any server.
