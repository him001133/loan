Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

Information We Collect

EduLoan Planner is designed with privacy in mind. All calculations and scenario data are processed entirely within your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal financial information.

Local Storage

Scenario data (loan comparisons you save) is stored only in your browser's local storage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible by our servers
  • Can be deleted by clearing your browser cache
  • Is not shared with third parties

Third-Party Services

EduLoan Planner does not integrate with external analytics, tracking tools, or third-party services that would collect your personal data. We do not use cookies to track your behavior.

Data Security

Since all processing happens in your browser, your data is inherently secure. No sensitive financial information is transmitted over the internet to our servers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting to this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us.
